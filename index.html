About
me

Panos is a Hydrogeologist & numerical modeller, with postgraduate studies in Hydrogeology.
For many years, he has worked in a wide range of related projects, in diverse hydrogeological environments, in Australia and Overseas (Western Australia, Europe and Africa), in the academic environment, in the groundwater industry, and in Government departments.
His professional experience, among others, includes the whole cycle of planning, investigation and development of groundwater resources, analysis, and quantitative interpretation and analysis of hydrogeological and hydrogeochemical data. Throught his career, he has developed some self-taught coding skills, which is seeking to develop further in this awsome Bootcamp course.
